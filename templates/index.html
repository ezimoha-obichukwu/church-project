Welcome to RCCG Ambassadors Of Chrict Teens Church

where we are committed to nurturing the faith of young people and equipping them for a life of purpose and service. Our church is a dynamic and inclusive community of teens and youth, where everyone is valued and encouraged to use their unique gifts and talents to make a positive impact in the world. We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of the Bible, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit in the lives of young people. Join us for vibrant worship, inspiring teaching, and engaging activities that will challenge and inspire you to grow in your relationship with God and with others.

Volunteer With Us.

We humbly invite you to join us in serving others and fulfilling God's calling to love our neighbors. By volunteering with us, you can become an instrument of His compassion and grace, touching the lives of those in need with kindness and generosity. Our mission is rooted in the values of faith, hope, and love, and we welcome individuals who share this vision and seek to live out their beliefs in service to others. As a volunteer, you'll have the opportunity to witness the transformative power of God's love in action, and to experience the joy that comes from serving others with humility and selflessness. May His blessings guide you in this noble endeavor.

I'm Interested

Join a Connect Group.

Joining a Connect group is a great way to grow in community, deepen your faith, and build meaningful relationships with others. Our Connect groups are designed to provide a welcoming and supportive environment where you can connect with others who share your interests, values, and life experiences. Whether you're looking for a Bible study, a prayer group, a social club, or a support network, we have a variety of groups to suit your needs. By joining a Connect group, you'll have the opportunity to engage in meaningful conversations, learn from others, and grow in your walk with God. Join us and experience the power of community today!
